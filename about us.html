About ACGCET Library

The ACGCET Library serves as a central resource for students, faculty, and researchers in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE). Our mission is to provide an enriched learning experience by offering access to a wide variety of academic resources, both physical and digital.

Our Vision

To cultivate an environment where students and faculty have access to knowledge that inspires learning and innovation in engineering and technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer comprehensive academic resources, foster a culture of continuous learning, and support the research and educational goals of our department members.

Library Resources

  • Thousands of academic books and journals
  • Online databases and digital resources
  • Specialized reference materials in Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • Collaborative study spaces for group projects
